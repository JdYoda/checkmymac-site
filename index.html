WHAT IT CHECKS

The full service-bench inspection, in one app

BATTERY

Cycle count, real capacity, condition and charging health on both USB-C and MagSafe — with the same thresholds a repair bench uses.

PORTS

Every USB-C and USB-A port tested live: plug anything in and watch the port light up. Dead and unstable ports have nowhere to hide.

SENSORS

Temperatures, fans, ambient light, lid angle, sleep sensor — read straight from the hardware, with anomalies highlighted.

TOUCH ID

Live sensor test that counts every finger touch — works even on a freshly wiped Mac with no fingerprints enrolled.

STRESS

CPU and GPU stress runs with a live throttling graph. A Mac that overheats under load reveals itself in minutes.

REPORT

A clean PDF verdict for every check — plus before/after comparison for repair shops, tied to a work order number.

HOW IT WORKS

Ten minutes from download to verdict

  1. Run it. No installation — download, open, and the app identifies the exact MacBook model and builds its checklist.
  2. Walk the checklist. Automatic probes read the hardware; guided tests walk you through screen, sound, keyboard and sensors.
  3. Get the verdict. Every check ends in a clear OK or a named defect — exported as a PDF you can send or print.

FAQ

Fair questions

Is it safe to run on a Mac I don't own yet?

Yes. The app is notarized by Apple, changes nothing on the machine, writes no fingerprints, installs no drivers and needs no internet. It reads hardware state and shows it to you.

Which Macs are supported?

MacBook, MacBook Air and MacBook Pro on macOS 10.15 Catalina or newer — both Intel and Apple Silicon. The checklist adapts to the exact model automatically.

Do I need the internet?

Only once, to activate the license. Diagnostics run fully offline — including on Macs that have never been online.

What if it's not for me?

14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Email support and the purchase is refunded.

Who makes this?

Yoda Service — an Apple repair shop in Bali. The app grew out of the checklist our own bench uses on every incoming MacBook.
